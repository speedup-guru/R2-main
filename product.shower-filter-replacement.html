How to replace your filter refill cartridge

Remove

Turn off the water. Open the filter housing and remove the old cartridge. 

Fit

Firmly press the new filter into the housing. Screw the cover back clockwise until it's secure.

Flush

Turn on the shower and let the system run for 1–2 minutes to flush out any loose particles.
