The Micro-Infusion System is designed to be simple, mess-free, and beginner-friendly. Each treatment takes just a few minutes and fits easily into your skincare routine.

  • Twist off the cap of the serum vial and screw on the sterile needle head.
  • Hold the device upside down for 1–2 minutes to let the serum flow into the needle head and saturate the area around the needles.
  • Start at the centre of your face and work outward, doing 2–3 passes in each area of your face.
  • Pat any leftover serum onto the skin. Leave on overnight for best results.
