Usage Instructions

How to Use:

Clean and dry your neck and décolletage to ensure the area is clear from any dirt, sweat, or skincare products.

Insert the USB-C cable from the Neck & Décolletage LED System into the port located on the top of the controller.

Secure the device around the neck to comfortably treat the entire neck and décolletage area. Alternatively, position the strap across the shoulders to lower the LED panel slightly and extend coverage further across the décolletage.

Press the ON/OFF button on the controller. The treatment will automatically run for 10 minutes and shut off at the end of the session.
