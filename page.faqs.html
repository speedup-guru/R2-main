Technology

Q-Rejuvalight Pro Facewear is a face mask that puts professional LED therapy in your hands so you can operate easily, all by yourself, in the comfort of your own home!

It comes with a compatible iOS/Android app that allows you to fully customize, track and plan your treatments.

Q-Rejuvalight Pro Facewear uses clinical-grade technology to deliver safe, concentrated wavelengths of light directly to the underlying layers of your skin.

The optimal wavelengths of light are absorbed by your skin cells and kick start the production of collagen, elastin, and fibroblasts.

In turn, this stimulates mechanisms within the skin that solve skin problems and concerns from the inside out.

The Q-Rejuvalight Pro has a total of 5 clinically-proven wavelengths of lights:

  • Infrared light (880 nm): Increases circulation, protects existing collagen and enhances product absorption.
  • Deep Red (660 nm): Accelerates skin healing to fade and prevent scarring. Builds collagen to help reduce wrinkles.
  • Red (630 nm): Stimulates collagen and elastin production, firms and plumps skin, repairs skin cells and promotes hydration to help reduce inflammation, redness and rosacea.
  • Amber (605 nm): Reduces appearance of dark spots. Soothes skin and calms swelling and inflammation.
  • Blue (415 nm): Anti-bacterial, eliminates and prevents acne breakouts. Soothes and calms skin.

There are three treatment modes:

  • The Pro-Aging Treatment
  • The Anti-Breakout Treatment
  • Custom zone selection on five facial areas via the Qure app.

Our serums come sealed with aluminum to ensure the serum stays sterile for treatment.

  • Firmly hold the ampoule bottle. Find the arrow on the cap lid and flip to open from the end where the arrow is located.
  • Once the seal has been broken, hold the cap and steadily bend the silver seal away from the serum ampoule until it almost snaps off.
  • Peel back the silver seal from around the top of the serum ampoule and dispose of it.
  • Remove the rubber stopper and pour the serum into your Micro-Infusion device.

If you’re mixing serums for your treatment, put back the rubber stopper on the vial to save the remaining serum for your next session!

Each needle head is to be used one time per treatment, and disposed of after each treatment. This minimizes the risk of infection and unnecessary damage to the skin. Use one full serum ampoule per treatment.

Not all serums are suitable for Micro-Infusion. We have developed our potent serum ampoules that are perfect for Qure’s Micro-Infusion System. They are formulated to have low risk of irritation and with no fragrance.

Once you’ve finished stamping your way to brighter and plumper skin, follow the below cleaning instructions:

  • Twist off the needle head with the cap attached and safely dispose.
  • Wash the chamber out with water and gentle cleanser.
  • Let the device air dry completely, on a clean surface, before packing away.
  • Open a brand new needle head packet and attach the needle head onto the device. Ensure hands are clean and don’t open the cap of the needle head.

Troubleshooting

If your Q-Rejuvalight Pro Facewear is not turning on, follow these troubleshooting steps:

  • Make sure the device is fully charged (you’ll see a blinking light when charging).
  • Remove the mask from the charging cable, as the mask cannot be used while plugged in.
  • Check that the USB-C charging cable is fully inserted when charging.

Still nothing? Try a different outlet or charging cable to rule out charging issues.

If the Q-Rejuvalight Pro Facewear is fully charged and still does not turn on, please contact us and our team will help futher.

Make sure that Location Sharing is enabled on your phone. Once confirmed, exit out of the app, and reopen - then connect to the Q-Rejuvalight Pro Facewear.

The Q-Rejuvalight Pro treatment is designed to shut off automatically after 3 minutes. It also may shut off after multiple repeated uses if it exceeds desired temperature. Allow the Q-Rejuvalight Pro to cool for several minutes and try again.

If the Q-Rejuvalight Pro is low in battery then it can shut off prior to the 3 minute treatment so ensure that the battery is fully charged.

If you're still experiencing the same issue and continues to shut off prior to 3 minutes, please contact us at support@qureskincare.com.

You may have been connecting the mask via the Bluetooth menu on your phone hence why this occurred. Please connect directly by opening the Qure app and connect to your phone bluetooth.
