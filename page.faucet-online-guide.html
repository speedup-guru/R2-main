How To Install

To install your new Faucet Filter, you’ll need the following:
  • Qure Faucet Filter with 360° nozzle
  • Multi-functional adapter (provided)
  • Adapter wrench (provided)
  • Bubbler wrench (provided)
  • Thread adapters (provided)
  • Silicone ring (provided)
Watch: How To Install

Step-By-Step Guide

Step 1
Remove your faucet’s aerator or rectifier using the bubbler wrench.
Locate the threading and identify if it is internal or external. If there's no threading, you’ll need the multi-functional adapter.
Step 2
Use the adapter that matches your faucet type (internal, external, or no threading).
Make sure the sealing ring is in place inside the adapter.
Step 3
Screw the adapter onto your faucet by hand first.
Use the wrench provided to tighten it gently (don’t over-tighten).
Step 4
Once the adapter is secure, twist on the Qure Faucet Filter. Make sure it’s snug but not forced.
Tip: You should hear 2 clicks when the Faucet Filter is correctly secured
Step 5
Let cold water run through the filter for 30–60 seconds to flush the system.
Done! You’re ready to enjoy filtered, skin-friendly water.

The Qure Faucet Filter is intended for skincare purposes only. It should not be used for drinking water or any other non-skincare applications. Replace the filter every three months for optimal performance. If you experience any issues such as water leaks or reduced flow, follow the maintenance guidelines in this manual.

How To Replace The Filter

We recommend replacing your filter every 3 months to ensure optimal filtration.

You may find that water flow slows sooner than this 3 month guideline. In this case, you can replace the filter sooner to maintain strong flow and effective filtration.

To replace the filter, you’ll need a new replacement filter cartridge.

Watch: How To Install

Step-By-Step Guide

Step 1
Turn off the faucet. Rotate the filter cartridge cover counterclockwise to remove the old filter cartridge.
Step 2
Peel off the seal from the new filter cartridge. Install the cartridge into the faucet housing.
Step 3
Screw the filter cartridge cover clockwise to secure it.
Step 4
Turn on the faucet and let raw water flow for 5 seconds, then switch to purified water mode and let it run for 3 minutes before first use.

Water Mode Selection

The mode selection toggle is located on the side of the faucet filter.

It is clearly labeled with two symbols: a Q for filtered water and a tap water symbol for unfiltered water.

Filtered water mode

When the dial is turned to the Q position, the water flows through the filter cartridge located at the front of the device.

The filtered water then exits through the lower outlet, providing clean, purified water ideal for facial cleansing and other skincare routines.

Tap water mode

By turning the dial to the tap water symbol, the water bypasses the filter cartridge and exits directly from the upper outlet of the device.

This mode is perfect for tasks where filtered water is not required, such as rinsing your hands or filling a container with unfiltered water.

Care & Maintenance

To ensure your Qure Faucet Filter works optimally, regular maintenance is important:

  • Wipe the filter’s surface with a damp cloth.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals when cleaning, and do not use steel wool, abrasive cleaners, or corrosive liquids.
  • Replace the filter every 3 months, or sooner if the water flow slows down.
  • Periodically check the connections to ensure there are no leaks.

Troubleshooting & Adjustments

Having trouble? Don’t worry, most issues are quick to solve.

Water leaks

Ensure that all silicone rings and adapters are properly fitted. If leaks persist, check the connections and tighten as needed.

Odor in the water

If you notice any unusual odors, flush the filter by running water through the system for several minutes.

Low or uneven water pressure

Low flow can occur if the filter is clogged. Remove and clean or replace the filter as needed.
