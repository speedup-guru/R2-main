Product Information

The All-In-One Serum brightens, plumps & smooths the look of skin using a unique blend of active ingredients:

  • Niacinamide clarifies and evens tone, refines pores, calms redness
  • Retinol and Bakuchiol boost cell turnover and collagen production, reducing fine lines, wrinkles, and pigmentation
  • Lactic and Mandelic Acids gently exfoliate to smooth texture and enhance radiance
  • Argireline® Peptides reduce wrinkle depth by up to 48 % in clinical use
  • Ethyl Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C) and Ferulic Acid brightens and protect against oxidative damage
  • Hyaluronic Acid delivers lasting hydration, plumping fine lines and smoothing skin

Usage Instructions

How to Use:

After cleansing, apply 1-2 pumps to cleansed skin (face and neck).

Use every second night during the first two weeks of use whilst your skin builds tolerance.

Use daily for best results.
