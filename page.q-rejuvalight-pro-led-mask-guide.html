Get started with Q-Rejuvalight Pro

Please read the complete instruction manual carefully before using the device.

The Qure Q-Rejuvalight Pro LED Mask is an at-home LED light therapy device designed to target fine lines, breakouts, and dullness in just one 3-minute treatment.

This guide will show you exactly how to use it, care for it, and get the best results from your treatments.

Quick Start Guide

Box out, mask on! To get started with your first treatment, you’ll need the following:
  • Q-Rejuvalight Pro LED Mask
  • Adjustable head strap (provided)
  • Eye protection goggles (provided)
Watch: Quick Start Guide

Step-By-Step Guide

Step 1
Set up your mask by attaching the adjustable strap.
With the logos facing down, slide the strap into the first clip. Loop the strap through the adjustor and second clip.
Step 2
Secure the strap with the provided pin on the inner side of the mask.
Step 3
Insert goggles from the inside of the mask.
Ensure that the smaller side of the goggles go in first.
Step 4
Prepare your skin for your treatment by cleansing and dry thoroughly.
Tip: Use our Dermal Mist to further cleanse and prepare your skin.
Step 5
Press and hold the power button ON for 2-3 seconds until the indicator light turns on.
Position the mask on your face. Adjust the straps to fit your face comfortably.
Step 6
Choose your treatment either in the Qure app or directly on the mask.
Press the power button once for Pro-Aging mode, or press it twice for Anti-Breakout mode.

Light sensitivity test: We recommend performing a light sensitivity test 24 hours before using this device for your first treatment. To perform this, apply the product to your inner forearm, ensuring the light shines directly onto the skin. Use it for a full 3-minute treatment. Wait for 24 hours. If you experience pain, discomfort, or redness lasting more than 2 hours, you might be light-sensitive. Please consult a doctor for advice before continuing use.

Charging Your Device

To charge your device, connect the supplied USB-C cord to your Q-Rejuvalight Pro and plug into a USB power adapter.

While charging, the indicator will flash white.

When the battery is fully charged, the indicator will be solid white. 

No light when plugged in means the device is fully charged already.

Watch: Charging Guide

Tips to maximize battery life:

  • Avoid leaving your device charging for long periods of time.
  • Avoid letting the battery completely run out.
  • Do not use your device while it’s connected to the charger.

Treatment Modes

The Q-Rejuvalight Pro Mask has two complete treatment settings: Pro-Aging or Anti-Breakout.

Press the power button once for Pro-Aging mode, or press it twice for Anti-Breakout mode.

Pro-Aging Mode

On pro-aging mode, red and infrared wavelengths work together to help support collagen production. This improves skin elasticity and reduces the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.

Anti-Breakout Mode

Our anti-breakout mode combines blue and red light wavelengths to target and eliminate acne-causing bacteria on the skin's surface.

Care & Maintenance

Wipe the mask down after each treatment.

Spray with our Dermal Mist to sanitize and keep the mask clean for your next use.

When not in use, place your mask in the luxury storage bag provided with your set. Store it in a cool, moisture-free environment.

Troubleshooting & Adjustments

Having trouble? Don’t worry, most issues are quick to solve.

Mask not turning on

  • Make sure the device is fully charged (you’ll see a blinking light when charging).
  • Remove the mask from the charging cable, as the mask cannot be used while plugged in.
  • Check that the USB-C charging cable is fully inserted when charging.
  • Still nothing? Try a different outlet or charging cable to rule out charging issues.

Mask turning off too early

The treatment auto-stops after 3 minutes per treatment. If it’s stopping earlier, your mask may need a full recharge.

Mask not connecting to the app

Ensure your phone/tablet’s Location Sharing is on. If connectivity issues persist, try closing the app and reopening to reload it.
