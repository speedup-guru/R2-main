Usage Instructions

Box out, mask on! To get started with your first treatment, you’ll need the following:
  • Q-Rejuvalight Pro LED Mask
  • Adjustable head strap (provided)
  • Eye protection goggles (provided)
Watch: Quick Start Guide

Step-By-Step Guide

Step 1
Set up your mask by attaching the adjustable strap.
With the logos facing down, slide the strap into the first clip. Loop the strap through the adjustor and second clip.
Step 2
Secure the strap with the provided pin on the inner side of the mask.
Step 3
Insert goggles from the inside of the mask.
Ensure that the smaller side of the goggles go in first.
Step 4
Prepare your skin for your treatment by cleansing and dry thoroughly.
Tip: Use our Dermal Mist to further cleanse and prepare your skin.
Step 5
Press and hold the power button ON for 2-3 seconds until the indicator light turns on.
Position the mask on your face. Adjust the straps to fit your face comfortably.
Step 6
Choose your treatment either in the Qure app or directly on the mask.
Press the power button once for Pro-Aging mode, or press it twice for Anti-Breakout mode.

Light sensitivity test: We recommend performing a light sensitivity test 24 hours before using this device for your first treatment. To perform this, apply the product to your inner forearm, ensuring the light shines directly onto the skin. Use it for a full 3-minute treatment. Wait for 24 hours. If you experience pain, discomfort, or redness lasting more than 2 hours, you might be light-sensitive. Please consult a doctor for advice before continuing use.

Treatment Modes

The Q-Rejuvalight Pro Mask has two complete treatment settings: Pro-Aging or Anti-Breakout.

Press the power button once for Pro-Aging mode, or press it twice for Anti-Breakout mode.

Pro-Aging Mode

On pro-aging mode, red and infrared wavelengths work together to help support collagen production. This improves skin elasticity and reduces the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.

Anti-Breakout Mode

Our anti-breakout mode combines blue and red light wavelengths to target and eliminate acne-causing bacteria on the skin's surface.
