Skincare is a lifestyle

Caring for your skin goes beyond the products you use. It’s about adopting habits that support your skin’s health every day. Our dermatologists recommend these expert tips to help you reach your skin goals even faster.

Cleanse and exfoliate regularly

Daily cleansing removes pore-clogging bacteria, while regular exfoliation with Beta-Hydroxy Acid (BHA) helps reduce irritation and prevents future breakouts

Keep your tools and surfaces clean

Makeup tools and your phone collect bacteria that clog pores. Cleaning them regularly can reduce the chances of breakouts and help keep your skin fresh.

Change your pillowcase every 2-3 days

To prevent buildup of oil, sweat, and bacteria that can lead to breakouts and irritation.
